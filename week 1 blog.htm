Herald college week -1

November 26th, 2022

Hello, I am Kapildev Chaudhary .After i passed my +2 examination I am decided to join Herald .It was started of my carrier to go to learn some new thing pursuing higher education.  It was my first time being in this level to study. I always wanted to be in this kind of university, as being a student i have always clear about my aim. my first day at Herald was great. All the student at first day are sitting in the big hall to at our orientation classes where we discussed about our collage associated with UK universities(the wolverhampton universities ) and here in Nepal largest group of ( ing) group. Here, we also discussed about our study pattern and there subject studied in different year. similarly about our assignment submission on time and lastly about our fees and the regular attendance on each and every module.

The day of class was 14 Nov 2022. The day of my semester that day i had so many things on my mind,  from what was I going to figure out where the class name and the floor it would be located at and what time does it start or how long it would be. It was full new experience. I am very excited at the prospect of devoting my long-term career to such a dynamic, fast advancing field. Ever since I was young, I have enjoyed dabbling with computers and experimenting with different applications. we have to study 4 module and they are as follows

1. Academic skills and team based learning

2.fundamentals of computing

3.Introductory Programming And Problem Solving

4.Web Technologies

so all we are learning about this modules in our first year and there is also others module we will be studying in different years. I found that the method of teaching in this college is very different from other college .The students find here a different and outer atmosphere which they lack in schools.
